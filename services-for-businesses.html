Why regional detail matters

A “Lake Norman” request can mean very different logistics.

A boat may be on a private lift in Mooresville, inside dry storage, tied at a marina, parked on a trailer, or located across the lake. The form should collect the exact service address or facility, access instructions, utilities, and any permission requirements.

Common access types

Where the boat sits changes what can be done.

01

Private dock or lift

Confirm safe walking surfaces, water, power, shade, equipment access, parking, and owner permission.

02

Marina slip

Confirm outside-vendor rules, insurance, check-in, water use, power, runoff, work hours, and whether the boat must be moved.

03

Dry storage

Confirm forklift scheduling, work area, boat movement, key access, utilities, and facility authorization.

04

Trailer or driveway

Confirm space around the boat, stable setup, drainage, hose and outlet access, neighborhood restrictions, and weather.
