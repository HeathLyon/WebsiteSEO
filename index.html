View all questions
How much does boat detailing cost?

Pricing depends on boat size, condition, service scope, access, and the preparation required for the chosen protection.

Can service happen at a driveway or marina?

Many providers offer mobile work where access and local rules permit it. Confirm water, power, environmental, and marina requirements.

Can polishing remove oxidation?

Light oxidation may improve with polishing. Heavier oxidation can require compounding, wet sanding, or more extensive restoration.

Ready to scope the work?

Start with the boat, the condition, and a few clear photos.
