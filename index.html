Before requesting a quote

Set the access and outcome expectations early.

Mobile does not mean every service can happen in every location. Bottom work, acid washing, wet sanding, and coating installation may require specific access, weather, lift, or facility conditions.

View all questions
Can detailing happen at my Lake Norman dock or marina?

Often, but the provider must confirm permission, insurance requirements, utilities, work space, environmental rules, and whether the requested process is allowed at that location.

Can a chalky or faded boat be restored?

Many boats improve through staged compounding and polishing; severe oxidation may require wet sanding, gelcoat repair, repainting, or a test spot before a result can be promised.

What photos produce a better quote?

Send both sides, bow, stern, interior overview, upholstery, floor or carpet, waterline, pontoon tubes or hull bottom if visible, and close-ups of the worst oxidation, stains, mold, scratches, or water spots.
