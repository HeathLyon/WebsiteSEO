About Boat Detail Match.

The site is designed to make boat-detailing requests clearer for Lake Norman owners and more actionable for independent providers.

◉

The idea

Make it easier to find the right level of boat care.

“Boat detailing” can mean a quick wash, a full interior reset, severe mold treatment, staged gelcoat correction, ceramic coating, pontoon-tube cleaning, or a multi-day restoration. A vague lead wastes time for both the owner and the detailer.

Lake Norman Boat Detail Match is structured to collect the useful details first: vessel type, length, location, access, condition, desired outcome, timing, and photos. That request can then be matched to an independent provider whose services and coverage fit the job.

03Transparent handoff

The website may

Support discovery and communication.

  • Collect and organize detailing requests
  • Route information to a selected independent provider
  • Track lead source, response time, and outcome
  • Publish educational service and regional information
  • Request honest feedback about the referral experience

The website does not

Replace the service contract.

  • Perform the physical detailing work
  • Set every provider’s final price or schedule
  • Guarantee restoration results or coating durability
  • Grant marina, dock, or property access
  • Substitute for provider licensing, insurance, or legal obligations

Before production launch
